Research

The Backbone of the Project

As we're replicating the existing web page for mobile, we prioritized analyzing the website's content structure, information types, and multimedia elements. This analysis will give us a clear understanding of how the content is organized and presented to users, guiding us in determining which elements are essential for the mobile app.

To ensure the design aligns with the established brand identity and maintains visual consistency, we'll use the same design guidelines, including colors, typography, imagery and speech.

Exploring Existing Solutions in the Market

The healthcare app landscape is brimming with tools to manage your well-being. Upon reviewing some popular apps in the field, we found that they offer a similar range of functionalities.

However, in addition to these basic functionalities, the apps also share common visual elements, such as a clean and modern design, effective use of negative space, consistent branding reflected in their color palette (often featuring calming blues and greens to promote feelings of well-being), and intuitive navigation.

Gathering My Insights

Based on research into user preferences and industry best practices, this app has been crafted to align with the needs and expectations of healthcare consumers. Here are key insights that have informed the design of this app:

Certain information sections, such as contact details, services, and appointment booking, are highly prioritized by users, indicating their essential nature for the mobile app.

Users prefer quick access to vital information without extensive scrolling, indicating a need for concise and organized content presentation.

The website contains extensive textual content that needs to be condensed for mobile screens, requiring careful selection of key messages and concise wording.

High-quality images and multimedia elements enhance user engagement.

Users expect intuitive navigation patterns similar to standard mobile apps, emphasizing the need for a clear and straightforward menu structure.

Consistency in branding elements, such as color schemes and typography, is essential for brand recognition and user trust.

Exploration of innovative features in competitor apps, such as integrated chat support or appointment scheduling, can inspire unique functionalities for the mobile app.

Designing for Consistency

Within the spectrum of colors already used in website elements, one of the main shades was selected to adapt to the app's design. This deliberate choice is deeply rooted in the pursuit of enhanced brand recognition and a seamless user experience.

Furthermore, the font mirrors the one used on the website, ensuring a cohesive and harmonious visual language. This consistency extends to the overall user experience, as users will encounter the same familiar typeface across both platforms, fostering a sense of ease and comfort while navigating the app.

Please note that the visual elements of the app may not perfectly mirror the current site design as updates may occur.

Test Responses

To assess usability and identify areas for improvement, I conducted user testing using the high-fidelity prototype with limited functionalities. Due to the project's personal nature and unfinished state, only five participants were recruited.

Each participant completed 3 standardized tasks designed for comparison. All participants successfully completed these tasks, which simulated common scenarios.

Conclusion

Reflection & Next Steps

The resulting design not only aligns with the clinic's brand identity, but it also delivers a seamless user experience. It offers intuitive interactions and empowers patients in their healthcare journey.

By providing a user-friendly platform for exploring specialties, accessing informative content, and facilitating communication with the clinic, this app empowers users to make informed decisions.
